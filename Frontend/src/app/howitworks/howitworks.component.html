HOW IT WORKS?

  1. Register yourself on the grraabit website by creating your user id and password.
  2. In order to register and participate in any deal, you will need to purchase "deal packs" which can be found on the "home page" or "my account page".
  3. Purchase the "deal pack" of your choice and complete the payment by using any of the online payment methods listed on the website.
  4. You can check your total balance and deal packs displayed under "My account."
  5. All "upcoming deals" are displayed on the home page as well as "My deals" page.
  6. You can now register for upcoming deals of your choice by placing the minimum required bids to proceed with the deal. Registration will be closed once required number of registrations have been met. Registered deals will be displayed under "My active deals."
  7. In case the number of minimum registrations are not met, the deal will be closed and rescheduled.
  8. Once the deal is live, you can place a bid by clicking on "Bid now". Each bid increases the price by "X" amount. A User is allowed a maximum of 10 seconds to place their next bid. Every bid placed resets the timer.
  9. If no bids are placed within the last 10 seconds, the highest bidder grabs the deal. The winner gets to purchase the product at the "winning bid price".
  10. All participants of the deal have the opportunity to purchase the product at "deal price".
